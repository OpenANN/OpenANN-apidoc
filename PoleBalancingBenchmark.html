Pole Balancing

This benchmark is based on the example Pole Balancing.

We compare the number of episodes that is needed to learn a successful policy. We use a Single Layer Perceptron (SLP) to represent the policy . In case of a partially observable environment, we estimate the velocities either with filters or by double exponential smoothing. We do 1000 runs per configuration.
