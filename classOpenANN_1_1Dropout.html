Detailed Description

Dropout mask.

The dropout technique tries to minimize similarities of neurons in one layer by randomly suppressing the output of neurons during training [1]. After training, we use the "mean network", i.e. we reduce the output of each neuron to compensate that all will be active.

[1] Hinton, G. E., Srivastava, N., Krizhevsky, A., Sutskever, I. and Salakhutdinov, R. R.: Improving neural networks by preventing co-adaptation of feature detectors, 2012.
